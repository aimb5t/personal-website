about


I help people and organizations answer the question 'what's worth doing next,' and build the quiet infrastructure that powers it.



My work spans research & design, consulting, and delivery — orienting and acting amid complexity by clarifying what matters, designing systems around it, delivering prototypes, and scaling solutions.



My focus is infrastructure at three scales:

  • Personal — Knowledge / information systems and tools for rich, self-directed lives
  • Cultural — Community-owned assets and informal institutions
  • Energy — Tech-led growth / innovation programs for energy and software companies

These converge at a critical edge:
AI, electrification, and individual sovereignty** where people are rethinking how they live, work, and build.

for fun
